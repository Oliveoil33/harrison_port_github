Photography

Photography is my passion. I can't get enough of it! I see the world through a lens, and every moment is a potential masterpiece. I can't imagine my life without a camera in hand, ready to capture life's wonders.

Portraitures

Portraits allow me to connect with people on a deeper level. It's not just about taking pictures; it's about telling their unique stories through my lens. I find immense joy in exploring different lighting, angles, and expressions to create stunning and evocative portraits. It's a form of artistic expression that speaks to my soul.

Couples

Couples photography is all about capturing the unique connection and love between two people. It's a privilege to witness and document those intimate moments. Photographing couples is a way of preserving their memories and allowing them to relive the special moments they share. It's a beautiful responsibility and an art I'm deeply passionate about.

Creative

Creative photography is where my imagination meets the lens, and I find endless inspiration in the boundless possibilities it offers. It's incredibly satisfying to experiment with various techniques, styles, and post-processing methods in creative photography. The possibilities are truly limitless.

Videography

Videography is my way of freezing time and sharing emotions. I thrive on the process of turning raw footage into a cinematic masterpiece. I'm fascinated by the power of visuals and their ability to evoke feelings.

Muted is a short film project I worked on in college. I operated the camera, directed the project, and worked alongside the editors. It is a comedy about two guys who sit on a remote and mute themselves and must figure out how to undo their mistake.

This student interview was conducted in college, and the interviewee was a classmate of mine. The whole class participated in the production of the interview. I operated the camera. I was then given all the video and audio files to edit my own version of the interview.
